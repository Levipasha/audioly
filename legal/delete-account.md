# How to Delete Your Account and Data

At Audioly, we believe in giving you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated information, please follow the steps below.

## Account Deletion Process
Currently, account deletion is handled manually by our support team to ensure all your data is correctly removed from our servers.

1. **Send an Email:** Compose an email from the address associated with your Audioly account.
2. **Subject:** Use the subject line "Request for Account Deletion".
3. **Recipient:** Send the email to **[YOUR_EMAIL_HERE]**.
4. **Processing:** Our team will verify your request and proceed with the deletion of your account, including:
   - Your profile information (Name, Username, Email).
   - All uploaded music files.
   - All friend lists and interactions.

## Data Retention Policy
Once your account is deleted, the action is permanent and cannot be undone. All files hosted on our secure cloud storage (Cloudinary) will be permanently purged.

---
*If you have any questions before deleting, please don't hesitate to contact us.*
