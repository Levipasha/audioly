# Privacy Policy for Audioly

**Last Updated: January 02, 2026**

Audioly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application ("App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** When you register for an account, we collect your name, email address, username, and password.
- **Profile Content:** You may optionally upload a profile image and provide a display name.
- **User Content:** If you upload audio files or metadata, we store those on our servers (via Cloudinary) to enable playback and sharing features.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **App Usage:** We may collect information about how you interact with the App, such as features used and music playback history.
- **Device Information:** We may collect basic device information such as your operating system version and device model.

## 2. Permissions
The App requires the following permissions to function correctly:
- **Media Library / Storage:** To play local music files stored on your device and to save/download content.
- **Notifications:** To provide playback controls in the system tray and send relevant updates.
- **Internet Access:** Required for account synchronization, cloud uploads, and streaming features.

## 3. How We Use Information
We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
- Manage your account and provide customer support.
- Facilitate social features like finding friends and sharing playlists.
- Personalize your experience.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use third-party services to power certain features:
- **Cloudinary:** To host profile images and uploaded media.
- **YouTube API/Streaming:** To provide music search and streaming capabilities. Please note that use of these features is subject to the YouTube Terms of Service.
